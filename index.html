ABOUT ME

I grew up on the southern coast of North Carolina. After graduating from East Carolina University, I moved to Charlotte and ran multiple multi-million dollar departments for Sam's Club, Harris-Teeter, and Target. I decided I wanted to get out of retail management and focus on a hobby of mine, coding. I had a basic understanding of HTML and CSS, but I had never gone beyond that. I completed UNC Charlotte's full-stack developer program in August 2017 and I want to use my new skills to help out my community and land a great job.
